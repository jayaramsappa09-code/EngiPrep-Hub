Question 1

What is the Meissner Effect?

Answer

The expulsion of a magnetic field from a superconductor during its transition to the superconducting state.

Question 2

What is a Fullerene?

Answer

A carbon allotrope whose molecule consists of carbon atoms connected by single and double bonds to form a closed or partially closed mesh (e.g., C60).
