Cookie Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Engi Prep Hub ("we", "our", or "us"). This Cookie Policy is designed to explain how we use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies on our educational platform located at https://engiprephub.in. By continuing to use our platform, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. We prioritize transparency and ensure our practices comply with relevant data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data—usually text files—placed on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work securely and efficiently, as well as to provide analytical information. In addition to cookies, we utilize modern browser local storage mechanisms to persist your specific educational preferences (such as your chosen dark/light theme and subject views).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our platform categorizes cookies and storage into essential, analytics, advertising, and personalization types. This detailed breakdown ensures you understand exactly what data is being utilized and for what purpose within our educational ecosystem.

4. Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with the core services available through our website. They cannot be disabled, as the platform cannot function securely without them.

  • Authentication & Security: We use Supabase authentication. Supabase issues secure session cookies to identify returning logged-in students and prevent unauthorized access to premium academic materials.
  • Local Storage: We use browser localStorage securely on your device to persist your UI preferences, such as the color-theme setting, ensuring a seamless visual experience when you navigate between syllabus subjects.

5. Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information that helps us understand how JNTUK R23 students use and interact with our platform. We utilize Google Analytics to track metrics such as:

  • Which subject notes are the most visited.
  • Geographical distribution of our student base.
  • Mobile vs. Desktop resolution to optimize our PDFs and visualizations.

This information is gathered entirely in an aggregated, anonymous form. We do not use analytics data to individually identify students.

6. Advertising Cookies

To keep the core educational materials (Notes, PYQs, Calculators) free for all engineering students, we partner with advertising networks such as Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our platform and other websites on the internet.

  • Personalization: Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve personalized ads.
  • Opt-Out: Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

7. Third-Party Cookies

In certain cases, we may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These may include embedded video tutorials, interactive CAD frames, or analytical scripts. We do not control these third-party cookies. We recommend reviewing the respective privacy policies of these third-party providers.

8. Managing Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can manage your preferences directly through your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from particular sites.
  • Block all cookies entirely (Note: blocking essential cookies will break login functionality and theme preferences on Engi Prep Hub).

9. GDPR & CCPA Rights

In accordance with GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California), we respect your academic data privacy. You hold the right to transparent information regarding tracking technologies. If you are a resident in a jurisdiction where these laws apply, you have the right to request the deletion of tracked personal data by contacting us directly, and you may withhold consent for optional analytical and advertising cookies via our consent management system or browser defaults.

10. Updates To This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our platform architecture, such as integrating new educational tracking systems, or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies, or if you need assistance managing your preferences, please review our full Privacy Policy or reach out to us dynamically via our Contact page. You may also email us directly at:
