Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Introduction

At EngiPrep Hub, we respect your privacy. This Cookie Policy explains how and why we use cookies on our website.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store information about your preferences, improve website functionality, and analyze traffic.

3. Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function properly (e.g., authentication, security). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics). We use this to improve our educational content.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve personalized ads based on your interests. Note: We do not serve ads on private dashboard pages.

4. Managing your preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our interactive cookie consent banner which appears at the bottom of the screen. You can choose to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences.

You can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings, although this may affect certain website functionalities.
