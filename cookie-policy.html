Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

EngiPrep Hub uses cookies for several purposes to enhance your user experience:

  • Authentication: Keeping you logged in securely via Supabase session management.
  • Preferences: Remembering your theme choice (dark or light mode).
  • Analytics: Using Google Analytics to understand how students use our platform so we can improve content.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests across the internet.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain website features, such as staying logged in or remembering your theme preferences.

4. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can change your preferences at any time.
