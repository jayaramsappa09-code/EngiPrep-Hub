Cookie Policy

At EngiPrepHub, we respect your privacy. This Cookie Policy explains how and why we use cookies on our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store information about your preferences, improve website functionality, and analyze traffic.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., login, security).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

Managing your preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie consent banner. You can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings, although this may affect website functionality.
