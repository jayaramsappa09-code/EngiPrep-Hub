1-Day JNTUK Board Exam Prep Recipe

Studying one day before the exam? Here is your exact tactical blueprint to score a high grade in the JNTUK R23 Data Structures paper using limited time.

Step 1: Numericals first
Lock 35+ Marks Instantly

Never start with long theory essays. Spend your first 40 minutes checking tree insertions, traversals, and hashing probe tables; they carry high weightage and involve guaranteed points.

Step 2: Drawing Map States
Pointers and Flowcharts

Always illustrate Linked List insertion/deletion, Stack push/pop, and Heap swaps. JNTU examiners award maximum marks to neat diagrammatic traces and logic flowcharts.

Step 3: Program comments
Readable Structure Explanations

Always write simple lines describing your pointer redirections like ptr->next = head. Adding comments proves authentic programmatic command.
