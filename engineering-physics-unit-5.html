Dielectrics polarizations; internal fields. Origin of magnetic moment, ferromagnetism, Superconducting transition.

Material Responses to Fields

Dielectrics polarize in electric fields. Magnetic materials align dipoles in magnetic fields. Superconductors expel magnetic fields perfectly.
