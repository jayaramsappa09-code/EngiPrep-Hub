Core Laser Principles

Absorption

Atom absorbs a photon ($h\nu$) and jumps from ground state $E_1$ to excited state $E_2$.

Spontaneous Emission

Excited atom jumps back to ground state randomly, emitting a photon of energy $h\nu = E_2 - E_1$.

Stimulated Emission

A photon incident on an excited atom forces emission of a second photon coherent with the first one.

Population Inversion

The state where the number of atoms in an excited energy state is greater than the number of atoms in a lower energy state. Required for laser action.

N₂ > N₁
