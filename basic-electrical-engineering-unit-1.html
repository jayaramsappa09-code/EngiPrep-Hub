Kirchhoff's Current Law (KCL)

The sum of currents entering a junction equals the sum of currents leaving the junction.

Kirchhoff's Voltage Law (KVL)

The algebraic sum of voltage drops and EMFs around any closed loop is zero.

Power Factor

The ratio of active power (W) to apparent power (VA). Equal to cos φ = R/Z.

Kirchhoff's Laws?

AC Quantities?
