Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Engi Prep Hub ecosystem.

What is Engi Prep Hub?

Engi Prep Hub is a premium educational platform tailored specifically for JNTUK R23 engineering students to prepare, practice, and perform in their semester exams with high-quality notes and interactive tools.

Is Engi Prep Hub free?

Yes, core features including subject notes, pyqs, and calculators are free. Premium interactive modules may require an account.

Which JNTUK regulations are supported?

We currently provide comprehensive end-to-end support for the JNTUK R23 regulation curriculum.

Are PYQs available?

Absolutely. We offer solved Previous Year Questions (PYQs) categorized by year and heavily analyzed for frequency to help you focus on high-yield topics.

How does the AI Professor work?

The AI Professor uses advanced natural language processing tuned for engineering concepts to explain topics, generate practice quizzes, and answer theoretical queries instantly.

How do I track progress?

By logging in, the dashboard records your streak, XP, and task completion rates to visually track your semester preparation.

Are Engineering Graphics tutorials interactive?

Yes, our Engineering Graphics lab tools provide animated visualizations, structural CAD matrices, and coordinate plotting mechanisms.

Can I use Engi Prep Hub on mobile?

Yes, the entire platform is responsive and mobile-first, allowing you to study anywhere on any device.

How often are notes updated?

Notes are updated per semester release, rigorously edited to align strictly with the latest JNTUK R23 syllabus modifications.

Is login required for notes?

Basic overviews are public, but access to full PDF notes, PYQs, cheat sheets, and XP tracking requires a free account login to prevent unauthorized scraping.

How do calculators work?

Calculators are built to solve explicit engineering problems, offering stepwise solutions for matrix eigenvalue checks, CGPA/SGPA conversions, and physics standard parameters.

Are viva questions included?

Yes, every subject includes an interactive Lab & Concept Viva module designed to prepare you for internal and external lab examinations.
