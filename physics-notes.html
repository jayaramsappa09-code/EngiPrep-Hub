Unit I & V • Bragg's Law

Diffraction Lattice Spacing

2d sin(θ) = nλ
  • d: Inter-planar atomic spacing (nm/Å)
  • θ: Glancing angle of diffraction
  • n: Order of diffracted wave maxima (1, 2, 3...)
Unit III • Hall Coefficient

Hall Field Carrier Density

R_H = 1 / (n * e)
  • R_H: Hall Coefficient constant (m³/C)
  • n: Charge carrier density density (density/m³)
  • e: Elementary charge constant (1.6 * 10^-19 C)
Unit IV • Optical Fiber Index

Numerical Aperture (N.A.)

N.A. = √ ( n₁² - n₂² )
  • n₁: Refractive index of active fiber core
  • n₂: Refractive index of fiber shielding cladding
  • Acceptance Angle: θ_a = arcsin( N.A. )
Unit II • Infinite Box eigenstates

Particle in 1D infinite Potential energy

E_n = n²h² / ( 8mL² )
  • E_n: Quantized energy state of electron
  • h: Planck's constant (6.626 * 10^-34 J·s)
  • L: Boundary width width of 1-D potential well
