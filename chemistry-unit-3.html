Question 1

What is a Reference Electrode?

Answer

An electrode with a well known and stable equilibrium potential, used to measure potentials of other electrodes.

Question 2

Why is Li-ion preferred?

Answer

It has high energy density, no memory effect, and low self-discharge compared to other batteries.
