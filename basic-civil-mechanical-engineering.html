II

Surveying & Foundations

Measurements & Bearing Capacity

Surveying Bearings Toolkit

Whole Circle Bearing (WCB)

Measured strictly clockwise from North (0° to 360°).

Quadrantal Bearing (QB) / Reduced

Measured from N or S, towards E or W (0° to 90°). Eg: N 30° E.

Shallow Foundations

Depth is less than or equal to width (D ≤ B). Types: Strip, Spread, Mat/Raft.

Deep Foundations

Depth is much greater than width (D > B). Used when top soil is weak. Types: Piles, Piers, Caissons.

III. Transportation

Pavements & Railways

Flexible Pavement

Asphalt/Bitumen surface. Multi-layered, distributes load through grain-to-grain contact. Lower initial cost, higher maintenance.

Rigid Pavement

Concrete surface. Acts as a solid rigid plate, distributes load via beam action. High initial cost, lower maintenance.

II

Manufacturing & Thermal

Engines & Processes

⚡ IC Engines Comparison

SI Engine (Petrol/Spark)

Uses Spark Plug. Otto Cycle (Constant Volume). Carburetor mixes air & fuel. Low compression ratio.

CI Engine (Diesel/Compression)

Uses Fuel Injector. Diesel Cycle (Constant Pressure). Only air is drawn in. High compression ratio.

Core Manufacturing Processes

🌋

Casting

Molten metal poured into a mold cavity to solidify.

🔨

Forming

Deforming metal via hammers/presses (Forging, Rolling).

⚙️

Machining

Removing material using a cutting tool (Lathe, Milling).

Power Transmission Drives

Belt / Chain / Rope Drives

Flexible drives. Used for long distance power transmission. Belts can slip, chains do not (positive drive).

Gear Drives

Rigid drives. Used for short distance, precise, high power, exact velocity ratio transmission without slip.
